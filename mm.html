The Gourde - Kusmi Tea

Marketing Mix

Sustainable and Ecofriendly pumpking pot

Product

Gourdes with World Cup Designs & Gourdes with Premade Aesthetic Designs, made in elegant and exotic styles to match Kusmi Tea’s current product aesthetics.

Convenience - Feature

Kusmi Tea consumers can simply mix hot water and tea compounds and then drink it directly afterwards. The usage of organic materials as its base acts as a heat isolator, which allows users to hold the Kusmi Gourde from all sides even without a handle.

Functionality - Design

The filters on the bombilla allow consumers to eliminate the necessity to drain away the tea compounds after mixing them in the calebasse because drinking from the bombilla filtrates the tea compounds so they do not enter the mouth of the consumers.

Sustainability

The gourdes are made of organic materials, which are dried up calabash fruits. We intend to create a production ecosystem where we collaborate with local agriculture firms and we purchase leftover calabashes from them to produce our Gourdes sustainably.

Product Line

Customizable Gourdes: Customization service such as patterns, colors, and engravings on their Kusmi Gourdes in the physical Kusmi Tea stores. Some customers would want to feel exclusive, so we can provide additional charged services for customizing their Gourdes.

Price

Cost-Plus Pricing Strategy: We will be charging €30.00 for a Gourde to earn a high profit margin for each sale.
Excluding labor and machinery costs, it costs approximately €1.00 - €2.00 for each fruit and €5.00 - €10.00 for each stainless steel bombilla straw.

Price Inelastic Demand

Due to the lack of presence in competitor brands, the Kusmi Gourde has low competition and alternatives, and it would be way more convenient for Kusmi Tea consumers to complementarily purchase the Gourdes while purchasing other Kusmi Tea products.

High Price at Launch

Launch the product at a relatively high price as it targets current and loyal customers of Kusmi Tea who have a higher tendency of purchasing the Gourdes as they are already invested in Kusmi Tea products.

Differentited Area inside Shops.

Distribution - Place.

Creating a differentiated space within the store for the gourde sales and customization, decorated with natural plants, vertical gardens, creating a natural environment, similar to a forest or jungle.

Increase in Sales

As our product is a complementary product, we are targeting already existing customers of Kusmi Tea, clients can feel curious and want to try the new product. These will be found within the shop.

Adding Value

Added value for customer as they could think of the product as high quality or luxurious.
Example: The Bloom, Madrid - Salvador Bachiller Restaurant.

Communication - Promotion

  • Sales Offers

    For the purchase of a Gourde, you get a free tea sample of whatever flavour the client wants. This way it promotes trying different flavours as clients do not actually pay them. It also enhances trying the product not only for the mate tea, but for any other flavour.

  • SPONSORSHIP - France’s Football Team

    Collaborate with France National football team (regarding the world football cup).

    Special pack where you buy the packet that has the Gourde with 5 servings of Mate Tea, and its packaging shows the football team. Promotes our products as healthier and energetic because they are being consumed by professional athletes and gives us great visibility.

  • Use of informative advertising within the Packaging

    It also acts as a coffee substitute, where mate tea also provides an energy boost but without the negative effects of tea. This is why many elite soccer players drink this tea before matches.

  • Direct Selling

    Taking advantage of the countless stores that our company has, we believe that it is better to opt for the direct sale of our product, since we achieve a higher profit margin, and we attract customers to our stores, increasing our visibility and recognition in the market.

    Also because the product is complementary so we are focusing at already established customers who we can make them buy the Gourde.
