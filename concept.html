The Gourde

The Kusmi Gourde is based on a calebasse, an Argentinian drinking bowl originally made in the shape of a coconut, equipped with a bombilla, a straw that filters water.

Without the Gourde: necessary to drain away tea compounds; have residual tea compounds on your drink, reducing comfort; lacks style exclusivity.

How it works: pour Kusmi Tea compounds and hot water into the Calebasse; mix the tea compounds and hot water firmly with the Bombilla; drink with comfort through the bombilla as it filters the tea water from the tea compounds.
