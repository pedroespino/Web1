Whom you market your products to, and how.

Segmentation

Geographic Segmentation.

First launch in the Kusmi Tea stores located in France because the highest concentration of consumers are in France.

The spirit of the World Cup occurring at this moment influences World Cup enjoyers to the Gourdes through the fact that football players often drink mate with these Gourdes. This also influences Healthy lifestyle.

Due to the influences of the celebrities and the famous people consuming Mate with the Gourdes, this would make the people wonder what they are and curious, eventually people will buy it due to the influences.

Psychographic Segmentation.

Targeting the lifestyle of consumers, we are satisfying their wants in terms of healthy lifestyle attributes.
